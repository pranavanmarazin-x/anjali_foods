Meet the Founder

Crafted with love by Anjeli

"Every recipe I make carries a piece of my heart — real ingredients, real care, real flavor."

Hi, I'm Anjeli — a passionate home cook turned food entrepreneur from the heart of Sri Lanka. What started as Sunday kitchen experiments with my grandmother's recipes soon grew into something much bigger: a mission to bring honest, preservative-free homemade food to every table.

From our hand-roasted nut mixes and fiery chilli pastes to our slow-cooked beef moussaka and flaky fish pies — every product is made in small batches with ingredients I'd feed my own family. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just real food.
